Shelter plans

We offer two tiers of experiences at the pet shelter: gold and platinum.


Here are the facilities your dog will get access to based on the tier:

gold tier

  • vegetarian meals (x3/day)
  • 24x7 access to drinking water
  • water wash (x1/week)
  • play time with other dogs (x2/day)
  • pricing:
    1. Rs/- 3000/week
    2. Rs/- 500/day

platinum tier

  • non-vegetarian meals (x3/day)
  • 24x7 access to drinking water
  • soap and bubble wash (x2/week)
  • play time with other dogs (x3/day)
  • pricing:
    1. Rs/- 6000/week
    2. Rs/- 1000/day
