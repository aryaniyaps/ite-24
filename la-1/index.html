About us

We are Chennai's largest dog oriented service with branches in Velachery, Adyar, Anna Nagar and Saidapet.
We have been providing the best training, utilities and shelters for pets since 1999.

We also provide training for newborn- adolescent and dogs, specializing in Indian dog breeds such as
Chippipaarai, Mudhoul Hound and Raaja Paalayam.

We also offer housing/shelter for dogs, with adequate facilities such as play areas and kennels.
